Gestures Module

class modules.gestures.arduino.Gestures(prober_instance=None)

Bases: object

Represents gestures of Fiona.

This class manages functionalities related to the robot’s gestures and movements, including the movement of its eyes, spoken interactions, and LED control.

Attributes:

ardu (serial.Serial): Serial connection to the robot. tnow (datetime): Current timestamp. told (datetime): Previous timestamp. det (bool): Indicates whether a face has been detected or not. ojov (int): Vertical position of the robot’s eyes. ojoh (int): Horizontal position of the robot’s eyes. speed (int): Speed of the robot’s movements.
